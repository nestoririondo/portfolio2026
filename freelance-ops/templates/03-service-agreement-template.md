- `[deliverable 2]`
- `[deliverable 3]`

## 3. Client Responsibilities

The client agrees to provide:

- Required text, images, logo and business information
- Access to domain, hosting, Google Business or other relevant accounts if needed
- Legal texts such as Impressum and Datenschutz, unless another provider is agreed
- Feedback within `[number]` business days where possible

Delays in client feedback or content delivery may delay the timeline.

## 4. Payment

Total project price: **`[price]`**

Payment schedule:

- 50% deposit: **`[amount]`**, due before work starts
- 50% balance: **`[amount]`**, due before launch, handover or transfer

Work starts only after the deposit is received. The website is launched or handed
over only after the final balance is received.

## 5. Revisions

Included revision rounds: `[number]`

Extra revision rounds or requests outside the agreed scope are quoted separately
and require written approval before work continues.

## 6. Changes to Scope

If the client requests new pages, new features, major design changes or additional
services after approval of this agreement, the provider will send a written change
order with price and timeline impact.

## 7. Ownership

After full payment, the client owns the final website files and final visual design
created specifically for the project.

The provider may reuse general know-how, code patterns, tools, components and
workflows that are not unique to the client.

Ownership transfers only after full payment has been received.

## 8. Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party services such as hosting, domains, email, forms,
analytics, fonts, CMS tools or payment providers.

Third-party fees, terms, outages or policy changes are outside the provider's
control unless explicitly included in the proposal.

## 9. Legal Texts and SEO

The provider can embed legal texts supplied by the client or an agreed provider.
The provider does not provide legal advice.

SEO basics may be included, but no specific Google ranking or traffic result is
guaranteed.

## 10. Cancellation

If the client cancels after work has started, the deposit is not refundable and
covers reserved time and work performed.

If work completed exceeds the deposit amount, the provider may invoice the
difference before transferring any work.

## 11. Portfolio Use

The provider may show the finished project in a portfolio, case study or social
post unless the client requests confidentiality in writing before the project
starts.

## 12. Care Plan

The first year of care is included unless otherwise stated in the proposal.

After the first year, continued care is **50 €/month** and includes hosting, domain,
SSL, technical updates, backups, basic uptime checks and small content changes.

New pages, new designs and new functions are not included in the monthly care fee.
